Konkonte with Wirewire Soup


konkonte with wirewire soup

Kokonte is a staple food eaten in many parts of Ghana, Togo and other West African countries.
It is primarily made of dried cassava flour.


Ingredient

  • Cassava Flour
  • Water
  • Wirewire (Niree)
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Meat or Fish

Steps:

    Preparing the Konkonte:

  1. Put Water on fire
  2. allow the Water to boil for about 15 minutes with the led tightly fit to the cooking pot
  3. Pour the Cassava flour into the boiling water.
  4. Stir for about 15 to 20 minutes until firm and sticky. also make sure the colour turns golden brown
  5. Dish out and serve.

    6. Preparing the "Wirewire" Soup:

  6. Dry fry the wirewire for about 10 minutes
  7. Blend or pound the wirewire until all turns powder
  8. Mix with water and strain
  9. after removing the chaff
  10. Pour the source into the stewing meat and cook for sometime
  11. Add tomatoes and still keep it on fire to cook for some more minutes.
  12. Remove from fire and serve hot.
