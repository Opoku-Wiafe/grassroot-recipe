MILLET DRINK (ZONKOM DRINK)


Millet Drink

Millet is a group of gluten-free ancient grains that offer several health benefits. It's widely consumed in developing countries throughout Africa and Asia. Millets are a group of cereal grains that belong to the Poaceae family, commonly known as the grass family.
There are several types of millets such as barnyard millet, foxtail millet, finger millet, pearl millet, proso millet, sorghum and little millet
I found a recipe for a millet drink called Millet Original Drink which is a healthy and gluten-free choice

Ingredients:

  • Millet
  • Ginger
  • Honey
  • Cloves
  • Black Pepper
  • Pepper
  • Sugar

Methods:

  1. Soak all ingredients in water and leave overnight.
  2. Blend the next morning and sieve the mixture until your desired texture.
  3. Add sugar or honey and refrigerate.
  4. Serve while cold with Fresh Bread or Freshly Baked Pie.
